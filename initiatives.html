Our Initiatives

Driving innovation and community impact through chemistry-focused programs that inspire, educate, and create positive change.

ChemFacts

The official communications magazine of CSIT
ChemFacts (Formerly known as Synthesis) is the official communications magazine of CSIT. It aims to bridge the gap between complex chemical concepts and the students through engaging articles, interviews, and visual content.

Journal club of CSIT

The Journal Club of CSIT is a platform for students to engage with current research in chemistry through discussions, presentations, and collaborative learning. It aims to foster a culture of scientific inquiry and critical thinking.

CSIT Alumini Database

The CSIT Alumni Database is a comprehensive platform that connects current students with alumni working in various fields of chemistry. It aims to facilitate networking, mentorship, and career opportunities for students.

CSIT Outreach Program

A platform for CSIT's outreach activities
The CSIT Outreach Program is dedicated to promoting chemistry education and awareness in the local community through workshops, school visits, and public lectures. It aims to inspire the next generation of scientists and foster a love for chemistry.
