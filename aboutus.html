About CSIT

Chemical Society of IISER Thiruvananthapuram (CSIT) is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering excellence in chemical sciences. Through various academic and outreach activities, we aim to create a platform for students to explore, learn, and contribute to the field of chemistry.

Our Vision

To cultivate a dynamic environment that promotes innovative thinking, research collaboration, and the advancement of chemical sciences while bridging the gap between academic learning and practical applications.
