About CSIT

Founder: Prof. J.N. Moorthy

About: Prof. J.N. Moorthy is the Director of IISER Thiruvananthapuram since April 2019. He is an organic chemist with interests in organic photochemistry, supramolecular chemistry, organic materials and mechanistic organic chemistry. He has been awarded the prestigious Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize and the J. C. Bose National Fellowship. He is an elected fellow of Indian Academy of Science (FASc.) and Indian National Science Academy (FNA), and a fellow of the Royal Society of Chemistry (FRSC).

Message from the Founder

Welcome to the CSIT student chapter. Our goal is to encourage curiosity, hands-on experimentation and communicate chemistry to a broader audience. We organise workshops, competitions and outreach events that blend fun with learning.
